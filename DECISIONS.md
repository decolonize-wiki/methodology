## v0.2 — promote `delegitimizing-labels` to a full category (2026-07-11)

**Ruling:** Accepted. Adds `categories/delegitimizing-labels.md` as the
seventh category; removed from `CANDIDATES.md`.

**Reasoning:** Met the promotion bar (platform repo's ANALYZE.md, §Methodology feedback) —
independently recorded in 4 articles by 3 adversarial reviewers (Scramble
for Africa, South Africa, Kingdom of Benin ×2), not coverable by amending an
existing category (it is the inverse of `euphemism`, and the occurrences
were cited to modern historians so `one-sided-sourcing` did not apply), and
now carries text-checkable "Flag when" criteria with "Do NOT flag when"
exclusions grounded in guha-1983 and mudimbe-1988. Passes the
hostile-historian test: the criteria require an established framing choice
(criterion, asymmetry, or pejorative term), not a contested interpretation.
Triaged from issue #1.
